Petit Chalet entre Amis

The events we've been organizing during 25 years, have been quite successful. Each week, each month, we continue to prove how popular our parties are becoming! For 7 years, the parties « Petit Chalet entre Amis », every certain Friday between April and September, have never stopped seducing the ones that are most rebellious to the « traditional » night life. We pictured that event as a party in a big country mansion. It immediately became the Rendez Vous of « Bourgeois Bohêmes » people of the western chic Paris. www.petitchaletentreamis.com

Aperitivo, grignotage et bavardages

During 5 years, every Thursday, from 7 p.m. until midnight, and widely inspired by what's going on in Milan, “Aperitivo, grignotage et bavardage” (drinking, chatting and nibbling!) is the Rendez Vous of those in their 30ies. No showing off, just laid back people having fun, discussing their week, planning their week end...
www.aperitivo-grignotagebavardages.fr
